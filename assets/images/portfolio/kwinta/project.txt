<p class="descriptive">Kwinta is a new brand of the Flemish Centre for Quality (Vlaams Centrum voor Kwaliteitszorg). Kwinta brings news and information on the topic of quality through innovation and talent. I was asked to design the Kwinta website in 2013.</p>

(picturefill: home-small.png class: screenshot alt: screenshot kwinta webdesign medium: home-medium.png large: home-large.png extralarge: home-xl.png)

<p class="descriptive">Of course the website was designed to scale seamlessly to the varying viewports of all kinds of devices.</p>

(picturefill: responsive-demo-small.png alt: web design kwinta medium: responsive-demo-medium.png large: responsive-demo-large.png extralarge: responsive-demo-xl.png)

<p class="descriptive">As this is in part a news site, the design focuses heavily on readability and ease of use.</p>

(picturefill: sluit-aan-small.png class: screenshot alt: screenshot kwinta webdesign medium: sluit-aan-medium.png large: sluit-aan-large.png extralarge: sluit-aan-xl.png)
